What We Do

At Seagull Tech.Point, we specialize in delivering innovative IT solutions, driving software development, and supporting digital transformation. Our mission is to empower businesses with cutting-edge technologies and create opportunities for future talent through hands-on internships.




What We Offer in Our Internship Program

Our internship program is designed to build practical skills and provide real-world experience, ensuring you are ready to excel in the tech industry. Here's how:

  • Hands-On Training: Dive into real projects, gaining practical experience and learning by doing.
  • Technical Skills Development: Master in-demand technologies such as:
    • Python: Develop skills in data analysis, automation, and backend development.
    • HTML & CSS: Build and design responsive, user-friendly web interfaces.
    • JavaScript: Enhance web interactivity and functionality.
    • SQL: Learn how to manage and query databases effectively.
    • Version Control (Git): Collaborate seamlessly and manage your codebase with Git.
  • Mentorship: Receive guidance from experienced professionals who help you refine both technical and soft skills.
  • Professional Networking: Build connections with industry experts and peers to grow your career network.
  • Certification: Earn a certificate of completion to highlight your accomplishments and enhance your resume.
  • Career Support: Access resume-building advice, interview preparation tips, and career planning insights.
